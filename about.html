Mental illness is predominant problem in our country right now due to post-pandemic stress, family, friends, school, and so much more. That is no excuse for resources for social welfare to be expensive. Many people cannot afford counseling and feel alone or misunderstood. That is why we created Ocean Waves, a soundscape platform to cater to individuals’ tastes for no cost. It’s absolutely free 100% guaranteed. No one needs to go through this alone, so we created this platform to let you know that you are not alone and that we are here to support you.
